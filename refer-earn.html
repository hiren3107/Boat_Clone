FAQ'S

Q. How do boAt Reward Points work?

+

boAt Reward Points are earned through our Refer and Earn program. When you make a successful referral, your friend instantly receives 100 boAt Reward Points, and you receive it once they make a purchase and their product gets delivered.

Q. How can I redeem my reward points?

+

You can redeem your boAt Reward Points during checkout while making a purchase on our website. Simply choose the option to apply your reward points and enjoy the discount.

Q. Is there an expiration date for boAt reward points?

+

Yes, boAt reward points expire after a period of 6 months from the date they are credited to your boAt account.

Q. Where can I use my boAt Reward Points?

+

You can use your boAt Reward Points exclusively on our official website when making a purchase.

Q. Can I transfer my reward points to a friend?

+

No, reward points are non-transferable and can only be used by the account holder.
